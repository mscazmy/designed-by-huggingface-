Frequently Asked Questions

I specialize in UI/UX design, web development, and branding projects for businesses and individuals who value thoughtful, intentional design. My sweet spot is working with clients who need a complete visual identity or digital product design.

The timeline for each project depends on the scope and complexity. A typical website redesign might take 4-6 weeks from discovery to launch, while a branding project could take 6-8 weeks. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

I typically work on a project-based fee rather than hourly rates. After discussing your project needs, I'll provide a detailed proposal with a fixed price quote. This allows for clear budgeting and eliminates any surprises.

I offer retainer agreements for clients who need ongoing design or development support. We can discuss a plan that fits your needs, whether it's a set number of hours per month or ad-hoc support as needed.
